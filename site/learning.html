Certified Change Management Professional (CCMP)
Prosci
Issued Apr 2021
ADKAR Change Management Prosci 3-Phase
What I Learned
That shipping a product is only half the job - the other half is getting people to actually use it. The Prosci 3-Phase Process (Prepare Approach, Manage Change, Sustain Outcomes) and ADKAR model (Awareness, Desire, Knowledge, Ability, Reinforcement) gave me a structured way to think about adoption, resistance, and reinforcement. Learned to build sponsor coalitions, run readiness assessments, and develop change management plans tied to real project timelines. Directly shaped how I approach rollouts, especially for internal tools and platform changes where user buy-in isn't automatic.

ADKAR Model Prosci 3-Phase Process PCT Model Sponsor Coalitions Stakeholder Engagement Resistance Management Readiness Assessments Adoption Strategy

Applied the Prosci Methodology to a real change initiative during the 3-day intensive, building a Master Change Management Plan with sponsor mapping, ADKAR Blueprints, communication strategy, and a resistance mitigation plan - ready for immediate stakeholder conversations.
