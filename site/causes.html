Causes

Organizations and missions I believe in

Big Brothers Big Sisters
Creating and supporting one-to-one mentoring relationships that ignite the power and promise of youth. For over 100 years, they've matched adult volunteers with children ages 4 through 18 to help them reach their full potential.
Why I Care
Mentorship changed the trajectory of my own life. The idea that one consistent, caring adult can reshape a young person's future isn't abstract to me - it's something I've seen firsthand. Every kid deserves someone in their corner.
Donate
St. Jude Children's Research Hospital
Advancing cures and means of prevention for pediatric catastrophic diseases through research and treatment. No child is denied treatment based on race, religion, or a family's ability to pay.
Why I Care
As a father of a thriving 4-year-old daughter, I'm reminded daily of what a blessing a healthy child is. Not every family gets that peace of mind. No parent should have to choose between their child's health and financial survival. St. Jude's model - where families never receive a bill - is what healthcare should look like.
Donate
World Wildlife Fund
Conserving nature and reducing the most pressing threats to the diversity of life on Earth. Working in over 100 countries to build a future where people live in harmony with nature.
Why I Care
This was the first cause I ever got behind - as an 8-year-old in Dubai, I organized a cleanup-the-beach drive. It's also championed by my hero, Sir David Attenborough, whose work taught me to see the natural world differently. Today it's a cause my daughter and I share. We're borrowing this planet from the next generation, and WWF operates at the scale the problem demands.
Donate
Habitat for Humanity
Bringing people together to build homes, communities, and hope. Working across all 50 states and in approximately 70 countries to create a world where everyone has a decent place to live.
Why I Care
President Carter's decades of hands-on work with Habitat - well into his 90s, hammer in hand - is one of the most inspiring examples of service I've ever seen. A safe home isn't a luxury, it's the foundation everything else is built on. Habitat doesn't just build houses, they build the conditions for families to thrive.
