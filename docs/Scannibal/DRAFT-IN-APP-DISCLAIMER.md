### Your Impact

Your Scannibal Supporter subscription fuels the DIA Fund.

**How It Works:**
- You pay for your subscription. Apple takes 30%. Scannibal's AI engines (Gemini + ElevenLabs) cost money to run.
- Everything else—what's left—goes to the DIA Fund, a family philanthropy supporting [CHARITY_NAME] and [CHARITY_NAME].
- You pick which one your subscription supports. You can change it monthly.

**What "Net Proceeds" Means:**
Net proceeds = your subscription fee − Apple's 30% − API costs. This amount is transferred quarterly to the DIA Fund.

**Transparency Built In:**
Every quarter, the DIA Fund publishes a full report showing: how much net proceeds arrived, where it went, and what impact it created. Both charities publish their own impact reports so you can verify the work gets done.

[DIA Fund Transparency Reports](https://thediafund.org) · [Charity Navigator](https://www.charitynavigator.org)
