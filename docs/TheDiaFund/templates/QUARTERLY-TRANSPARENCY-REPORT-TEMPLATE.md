## Report Footer

**About The DIA Fund**

The DIA Fund (Dream. Inspire. Advance.) is a family philanthropy initiative dedicated to hunger relief, animal welfare, environmental protection, and expanding opportunity for children. Named for Dia Rao, the fund serves as a multi-decade teaching tool and documented legacy of generosity.

**How It Works**

Every subscription to [Scannibal](https://scannibal.app) generates net proceeds that flow directly to The DIA Fund. These proceeds are held and recommended as grants by the Rao family, with Dia learning to lead this work as she grows.
