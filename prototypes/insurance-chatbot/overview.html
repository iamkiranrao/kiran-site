Insurance AI Assistant

A chatbot that walks you through filing an insurance claim without losing track of anything. It remembers what you've said, asks the right follow-ups, and handles the whole process from start to finish.

LangGraph LangChain State Machine Python Flask NLP
Launch Prototype

The Problem

Insurance companies answer the same questions millions of times - filing claims, looking up policies, sorting out billing. Most chatbots make you start over every time you ask something new. You explain your situation, get handed off, and explain it all again. This prototype keeps track of the whole conversation so you never have to repeat yourself.

System Architecture

Most chatbots follow a rigid script - if the conversation goes sideways, they're lost. This one uses LangGraph to manage the conversation in stages. Each stage knows what happened before it, so the bot can handle complex, multi-step requests without dropping the thread.

Why LangGraph over Dialogflow? Dialogflow is great for simple Q&A. But insurance claims aren't simple - they're multi-step, branching conversations. LangGraph remembers what you said three steps ago. Dialogflow doesn't.
