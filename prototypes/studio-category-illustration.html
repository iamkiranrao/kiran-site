National Parks Poster Series

12 posters · same dimensions, mid-century travel-poster aesthetic

What this iteration adds

Series-as-card pattern. The National Parks card sits in the masonry as a single tile with a 2×2 mini-preview of 4 posters and a "Series · 12" pill. Subtle stacked-card shadow signals "more inside." Click → lightbox opens in series view showing the full 12-poster uniform grid (since all posters are the same 2:3 portrait dimension).

Uniform grid inside the series. When you open the series, masonry collapses to a fixed 4-column grid because all pieces share dimensions — exactly as you described. Click any individual poster → lightbox swaps to single-piece view with a "← Back to series" button so you can return to the grid. Close button (×) or ESC exits everything.

Two click depths. Category → Series → Individual piece. Each level has a clean way back. Series view feels like opening a folder; single view feels like the lightbox we already had.
