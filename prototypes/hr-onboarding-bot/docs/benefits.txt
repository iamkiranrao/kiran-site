EMPLOYEE BENEFITS PACKAGE
NovaTech Corporation Comprehensive Benefits Guide

OVERVIEW

NovaTech Corporation provides a competitive and comprehensive benefits package designed to support the health, wellbeing, and professional development of our employees and their families. Benefits commence from your first day of employment, unless otherwise specified.

HEALTH INSURANCE

All full-time employees and their eligible dependents receive health insurance coverage through Medibank, one of Australia's leading health insurance providers. The coverage includes:

Hospital Insurance: Coverage for private hospital admission, including accommodation, theatre fees, and medical services. The plan covers 80% of out-of-hospital medical expenses.

Extras Insurance: Dental, optical, physiotherapy, chiropractic, and other allied health services are covered. The standard extras package provides coverage for preventative dental (100% of major services), optical (frames and lenses up to $400 per 24 months), and up to 20 visits per annum to allied health practitioners.

Your Medibank membership card is provided upon employment and can be used immediately. Dependents and family members may be added to your policy at a cost, with details available from HR. To make any changes to your health insurance coverage, contact Medibank directly on 1300 13 23 13 or visit www.medibank.com.au.

WELLNESS ALLOWANCE

All employees receive an annual wellness allowance of $1,500 to support their physical and mental health. This allowance can be used for:
- Gym or fitness studio memberships
- Yoga, pilates, or fitness classes
- Mental health counseling or therapy (not covered by health insurance)
- Wellness retreats or workshops
- Wellness equipment (standing desks, ergonomic chairs, meditation cushions)
- Nutrition or health coaching

Wellness allowance is credited in January each year and is non-accumulative. Unused allowance does not roll over. To use your wellness allowance, submit receipts to HR@novatech.com with a brief description of the expense for reimbursement.

LEARNING & DEVELOPMENT BUDGET

We invest in our people's professional growth. All employees receive an annual learning and development budget of $2,000. This budget can be used for:
- Online courses (e.g., Coursera, LinkedIn Learning, Pluralsight)
- Professional certifications (AWS, Google Cloud, security certifications, etc.)
- University or diploma courses
- Industry conferences and workshops
- Books and learning materials
- Coaching and mentoring

To use your L&D budget, discuss proposed courses with your manager to ensure alignment with your role and career goals. Managers typically approve requests within 3-5 business days. Learning and development activities undertaken during work hours may be approved as paid time off. Unused L&D budget does not roll over to the next year, though exceptions may be made for long-term certification programs with manager approval.

SALARY SACRIFICE OPTIONS

NovaTech offers salary sacrifice arrangements that allow you to reduce taxable income by directing a portion of your pre-tax salary toward eligible expenses. Current salary sacrifice options include:

Superannuation: Contribute additional amounts to your super fund via salary sacrifice. This reduces your taxable income and increases retirement savings.

Vehicle Novated Lease: Salary sacrifice vehicle lease payments, including fuel, maintenance, and insurance through approved providers.

Technology Equipment: Salary sacrifice laptop, mobile phone, or other approved technology equipment to support remote work.

Income Protection Insurance: Salary sacrifice premium payments for income protection insurance.

To establish a salary sacrifice arrangement, contact Finance at finance@novatech.com. Salary sacrifice arrangements typically begin the month following approval.

EMPLOYEE ASSISTANCE PROGRAM (EAP)

NovaTech provides access to an Employee Assistance Program (EAP) through Assure, offering confidential counseling and support services to you and your immediate family members at no cost. The EAP includes:

- Unlimited confidential counseling sessions (up to 6 sessions per issue, per year)
- Mental health support and stress management
- Legal consultation (4 hours per year)
- Financial counseling
- Family and relationship support
- Crisis intervention services (24/7 availability)

To access EAP services, call Assure on 1300 112 173 or visit www.assurewellbeing.com.au. Your identity and use of services remain completely confidential.

GYM MEMBERSHIP DISCOUNT

All employees receive a discounted gym membership through our partnership with Fitness First and Anytime Fitness. Eligible members receive:
- 20% discount on monthly membership fees
- Unlimited access to all participating locations nationwide
- No joining or cancellation fees
- Access to group fitness classes and personal training (at additional cost)

To activate your gym membership discount, contact HR@novatech.com with your preferred location and provider.

PROFESSIONAL MEMBERSHIPS

NovaTech will contribute up to $500 per annum toward professional membership fees for industry bodies relevant to your role (e.g., Australian Computer Society, relevant engineering associations). Submit your membership details to HR for approval.

PARENTAL SUPPORT SERVICES

Employees with dependent children may access subsidized childcare partnerships and backup childcare services to support work-life balance. Details and provider information are available from HR.

ADDITIONAL BENEFITS

- Death and Disability Insurance: Automatic cover of 2x annual salary (spouse/dependents eligible for benefits)
- Company Intranet Access: Subsidized mobile phone plans and discounted broadband (eligible staff)
- Volunteer Time: 1 day per annum paid leave for volunteering with not-for-profit organizations

For questions about benefits or to discuss individual circumstances, contact Human Resources at hr@novatech.com.
