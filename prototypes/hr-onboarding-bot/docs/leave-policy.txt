LEAVE ENTITLEMENTS POLICY
NovaTech Corporation Human Resources

OVERVIEW

NovaTech Corporation is committed to supporting the wellbeing of our employees by providing comprehensive leave entitlements. This policy outlines all leave types available to full-time employees and the processes for requesting and managing leave.

ANNUAL LEAVE

All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year. Annual leave accrues from your first day of employment and is calculated on a pro-rata basis for employees who join partway through the year.

Annual leave can be taken at times mutually agreed between the employee and their manager. We encourage employees to take their leave to maintain work-life balance and prevent burnout. Unused annual leave does not automatically carry over to the next year; however, employees may carry forward up to 5 days of unused leave with manager approval. Any leave in excess of this carryover limit is forfeited at the end of the calendar year.

Upon termination of employment, employees are entitled to payment for all accrued but untaken annual leave.

PERSONAL/SICK LEAVE

Full-time employees receive 10 days of paid personal and sick leave per calendar year. This leave is intended to cover:
- Personal illness or medical appointments
- Care of family members who are ill
- Unexpected personal emergencies
- Public holidays (when required to work)

Sick leave is provided on a non-accumulating basis. Employees should notify their manager as soon as practicable when taking unplanned sick leave. For absences of more than three consecutive days, a medical certificate must be provided.

PARENTAL LEAVE

NovaTech Corporation provides paid parental leave to support growing families:

Primary Carer Leave: 16 weeks of paid leave for the primary carer (birth parent or primary adoptive parent). This leave is typically taken immediately following the birth or adoption of a child, though timing may be flexible with manager approval.

Secondary Carer Leave: 6 weeks of paid leave for the secondary carer (partner or co-parent). This leave may be taken during the same period as primary carer leave or at a later date, at the preference of the employee and their partner.

Employees may return to work on a part-time or flexible basis following parental leave return. Return dates and arrangements should be discussed with your manager and HR at least 4 weeks in advance. Job protection is guaranteed for the duration of parental leave.

COMPASSIONATE LEAVE

Employees are entitled to 3 days of paid compassionate leave per occasion to deal with the death or serious illness of an immediate family member (spouse, de facto partner, child, parent, or sibling). Additional unpaid leave may be negotiated with your manager and HR for situations requiring extended time.

STUDY LEAVE

NovaTech Corporation supports employee development through paid study leave. Employees may request up to 5 days of paid study leave per calendar year for:
- University or professional qualification studies
- Professional development courses
- Industry conferences or training
- Certification exams (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud, professional certifications)

Study leave must be pre-approved by your manager. Study leave supporting NovaTech's business needs (e.g., cloud certifications relevant to your role) may be approved more readily.

LEAVE REQUEST PROCESS

All leave requests must be submitted through BambooHR, our Human Resources Information System. To request leave:

1. Log in to BambooHR using your NovaTech credentials
2. Navigate to the "Time Off" section
3. Click "Request Time Off" and select the leave type
4. Enter the dates and duration of leave requested
5. Include any relevant notes or context
6. Submit for manager approval

Your manager will respond to your request within 2 business days. For annual leave, requests should be made at least 2 weeks in advance. Emergency or unplanned leave should be communicated to your manager immediately via phone or message, with a formal BambooHR entry to follow as soon as practical.

Approved leave is visible in your manager's calendar and on the team scheduling view in BambooHR. All leave taken is recorded and tracked against your annual entitlements.

LEAVE DURING COMPANY SHUTDOWNS

NovaTech typically observes a company shutdown period around Christmas and New Year (typically December 24 - January 2), where minimal operations are maintained. Annual leave taken during this period counts toward your annual entitlement. Employees required to work during shutdown receive penalty rates or time in lieu as appropriate.

POLICY COMPLIANCE

Misuse of leave entitlements may result in disciplinary action. Providing false information to obtain leave is considered misconduct.

For questions about leave entitlements or to discuss specific circumstances, contact Human Resources at hr@novatech.com or ext. 5500 (Sydney).
