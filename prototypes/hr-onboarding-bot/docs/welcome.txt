WELCOME TO THE NOVATECH CORPORATION FAMILY

We're thrilled to have you join NovaTech Corporation. This handbook is designed to help you understand our company culture, values, and the support systems we have in place to help you thrive in your role.

OUR COMPANY MISSION

NovaTech Corporation exists to transform digital experiences through innovative technology solutions. We believe that great software starts with great people, and we're committed to creating an environment where creativity, collaboration, and continuous learning flourish. Our mission is to deliver cutting-edge technology products that solve real-world problems while maintaining the highest standards of quality and integrity.

OUR CORE VALUES

Integrity: We conduct business with honesty and transparency in all our dealings.
Innovation: We embrace bold thinking and encourage calculated risk-taking to drive progress.
Collaboration: We believe great solutions come from diverse teams working together.
Excellence: We hold ourselves to the highest standards in everything we do.
Respect: We value and celebrate the diverse backgrounds and perspectives of our team members.

OFFICE LOCATIONS & FACILITIES

NovaTech Corporation operates from three major office locations, strategically positioned across the Asia-Pacific region:

Sydney Headquarters (NSW, Australia)
Our primary headquarters is located in the heart of Sydney's tech district. This is our largest facility and serves as the company hub, hosting our executive team, product development, and main support services. The Sydney office features collaborative open spaces, dedicated meeting rooms, a company café with complimentary beverages, and wellness facilities including a meditation room and standing desk options.

Melbourne Office (VIC, Australia)
Our Melbourne office houses specialized teams focused on client services and regional operations. The Melbourne facility offers modern workspaces with team collaboration areas and access to the local tech community. Regular team-building events and knowledge-sharing sessions occur at this location.

Singapore Regional Office
Our Singapore office serves as our Asia-Pacific hub, supporting our expansion throughout the region. This facility enables us to serve our growing customer base in Southeast Asia and maintains 24/7 support capabilities for our global clients.

GETTING STARTED

Your first week at NovaTech is dedicated to onboarding and integration into our team. Your manager will provide you with an onboarding schedule that includes orientation sessions, IT setup, team introductions, and introductory training on our key systems and processes. Don't hesitate to reach out if you have questions—we encourage a culture of curiosity and support.

For any questions about this handbook or our policies, please contact the Human Resources team at hr@novatech.com or visit the HR section of our internal portal.
